A series of practical tutorials on running multilevel models:

  • Jeroen Ooms has made a series of video tutorials which cover the material in chapter 2 of Joop J. Hox's Multilevel Analysis book: setting up a null model, gradually adding fixed-effects and random-effects, adding cross-level interactions. The great value in these is that they are available for HLM, R, SPSS, MLwin or MPlus (Stata is not among them). They are a bit old (from 2009, in fact), but they are quite informative.
