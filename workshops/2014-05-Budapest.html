Materials from a very short, 2-hour workshop on the basics of multilevel modeling (terminology, notation, fixed- and random-effects, cross-level interactions), at the Political Behavior Research Group (PolBeRG), Central European University, Budapest, on May 19. The script includes comments with variable descriptions and how to set-up the folder structure before running the script.
