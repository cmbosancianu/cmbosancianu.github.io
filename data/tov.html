The data has been assembled primarily between 2014 and 2016, with the purpose of using it for my PhD dissertation. It is based on the "True European Voter" project, in the sense of adopting the same coding scheme and sourcing 9 countries from it: Germany, Greece, Iceland, Italy, Norway, Portugal, Spain, Sweden, and Switzerland. To these, a further 12 countries were merged by myself: Australia, Belgium, Canada, Denmark, Finland, France, Israel, Japan, Netherlands, New Zealand, the United Kingdom, and the United States. Collectively, these 21 countries comprise the True OECD Voter (TOV) project.

As I am not allowed to share either the raw True European Voter data, or the individual country series, the TOV consists of a series of .R scripts which will perform the cleaning, recoding, and merging of the data.
