As part of some of the projects I have been involved in, I have assembled a few data sets which are made available here free to download and use provided that they are properly attributed. If, in certain instances, I am not allowed to post the raw data due to copyright issues, the "data set" might represent R code for merging the raw data which then needs to be obtained by the user.

  • "True OECD Voter" - the data set extends the "True European Voter" (TEV) project to cover a number of OECD countries (United States, Japan, New Zealand, Australia, Canada, Israel, plus a full series for the United Kingdom). [updated June 5, 2017] As the TEV project has not been launched yet, the R scripts that perform the merging will be uploaded at a later date.
